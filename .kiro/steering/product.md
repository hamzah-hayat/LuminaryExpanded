# Product Overview

Luminary Expanded is a Stellaris mod that overhauls the Galactic Paragons DLC's Luminary origin. It adds new events, situations, council agendas, milestone achievements, and expanded player choices to make the origin more engaging.

Key features:
- Dying Heir situation (bidirectional situation with three treatment approaches)
- Six milestone achievement events tied to empire accomplishments
- Luminary trait max cap at 20 with a "Legendary Luminary" event
- Quality-of-life improvements (ethic choice, heir customization, 40 Year Plan choices)
- Expanded Forgotten situation events

The mod targets Stellaris v4.3.* and requires the Galactic Paragons DLC. It overwrites `events/origin_events_paragon.txt` and `common/traits/10_paragon_traits.txt` from vanilla.
